## üì± Installation Steps (Step-by-Step)

### For Android Phones/Tablets:

#### Step 1: Enable "Install from Unknown Sources"

Before installing, you need to allow your phone to install apps from sources other than Google Play Store:

**For Android 8.0 and above:**
1. Go to **Settings** on your phone
2. Tap **Security & Privacy** (or just **Security**)
3. Look for **Install unknown apps** or **Unknown sources**
4. Find your **File Manager** or **Browser** in the list
5. Toggle it **ON** to allow installations

**For Android 7.0 and below:**
1. Go to **Settings**
2. Tap **Security**
3. Find **Unknown sources** and toggle it **ON**
4. Confirm by tapping **OK**

#### Step 2: Transfer the APK to Your Phone

**Method 1: Email (Easiest)**
1. Email the `DemoTrading-v1.0.apk` file to yourself
2. Open the email on your Android phone
3. Download the APK file

**Method 2: USB Cable**
1. Connect your Android phone to computer via USB
2. Copy `DemoTrading-v1.0.apk` to your phone's **Downloads** folder
3. Disconnect the USB cable

**Method 3: Cloud Storage**
1. Upload `DemoTrading-v1.0.apk` to Google Drive, Dropbox, or similar
2. Open the cloud storage app on your phone
3. Download the APK file

**Method 4: Direct Transfer (if nearby)**
1. Use Bluetooth, WiFi Direct, or Nearby Share
2. Send the APK file to your Android device

#### Step 3: Install the App

1. Open your **File Manager** (or **My Files**) app on your phone
2. Navigate to **Downloads** folder (or wherever you saved the APK)
3. Tap on `DemoTrading-v1.0.apk`
4. You'll see a screen asking "Do you want to install this application?"
5. Tap **Install**
6. Wait for installation to complete (takes 10-20 seconds)
7. Tap **Open** to launch the app immediately, or tap **Done**

#### Step 4: Launch the App

1. Find the **Demo Trading** app icon on your home screen or app drawer
2. Tap the icon to open
3. The app will load (first launch may take 5-10 seconds)
4. You're ready to explore! üéâ

## üîç Troubleshooting

### Issue: "App not installed" Error

**Solutions:**
- Make sure you have at least 200 MB free space
- Uninstall any previous version of the app
- Restart your phone and try again
- Check if your Android version is 8.0 or higher

### Issue: "Parse Error" or "There was a problem parsing the package"

**Solutions:**
- Make sure the APK file downloaded completely (should be 125 MB)
- Re-download the APK file
- Try transferring via a different method

### Issue: App crashes on launch

**Solutions:**
- Clear the app cache: Settings ‚Üí Apps ‚Üí Demo Trading ‚Üí Clear Cache
- Restart your phone
- Make sure your Android version is compatible
- Ensure you have stable internet connection (for first launch)

### Issue: "For security, your phone is not allowed to install unknown apps from this source"

**Solution:**
- You need to enable "Unknown sources" for your File Manager or Browser
- Go back to **Step 1** and follow the instructions carefully
- Different phone brands (Samsung, Xiaomi, Oppo) may have slightly different settings

### Issue: Charts not responding to touch

**Solutions:**
- Make sure you're touching directly on the chart area
- Try tapping and dragging slowly across the chart
- Restart the app if charts freeze
